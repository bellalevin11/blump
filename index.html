About BLUMP

BLUMP (Eli Blumkin) is a Boston-born DJ with a background in piano and drums, blending musical instinct with house beats. He brings groovy house, disco edits, and nostalgic EDM remixes to life, energizing dance floors from Copenhagen to St. Louis. His sets aim to bring back the infectious grooves that the dancefloors deserve.
